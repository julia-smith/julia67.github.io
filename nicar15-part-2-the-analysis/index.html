#NICAR15 Part 2: A Review

18 March 2015 on opennews, nicar

This is Part 2 of my comments on NICAR 2015, where I discuss my overall thoughts on the conference. If you want to read about specific sessions, see Part 1.

So #NICAR15 is cool. Super fun meeting people I previously thought only existed on Twitter.

— Julia Smith (@julia67) March 5, 2015


Some overall thoughts

Conference set up

I liked the way NICAR was organized. I thought sessions were the right length of time, and there was good variety in types of sessions (hands-on vs panel vs demo). The breaks between sessions seemed appropriate, and I liked how the full conference session was dedicated to lightening talks rather than a keynote speaker.

Julia
